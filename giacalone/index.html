Significant Sales

Record-Breaking
$62M Naples Sale

In 2022, Paul Arpin of Premier Sotheby's International Realty orchestrated a landmark transaction in Naples real estate — representing both buyer and seller in the $62 million sale of a 16,000+ sq. ft. beachfront estate at 25 16th Ave. S., setting a new record for single-family residential properties and surpassing the previous record by $10 million.

The six-bedroom estate on 1.5 acres off Gulf Shore Boulevard features dual pools — including a second-floor infinity-edge pool with panoramic Gulf views — lush landscaping, an exercise wing, and a three-car garage.
