About the Neighborhood

The Heart of Naples

A gorgeous Gulf Coast gem that takes locals and visitors back in time, Old Naples encompasses a unique offering of Naples real estate. From quaint cottages and bungalows to exquisite estates, Old Naples homes represent a blend of elegant charm and sophistication.

Enhanced by stunning scenery, these remarkable residences serve as resplendent retreats during the winter months or make for excellent full-time escapes. While the natural beauty of the area exudes tranquility and seclusion, the ample amenities of downtown Naples are moments away. For example, the world-famous Fifth Avenue South invites residents and visitors alike to indulge in eclectic dining and shopping.
