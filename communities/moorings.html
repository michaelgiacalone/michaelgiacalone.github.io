About the Neighborhood

Timeless Coastal Living

Centrally located between Coquina Sands and Park Shore, Moorings real estate offers convenient coastal living featuring waterfront properties with private boat docks and shimmering views in a sought-after luxury community. Moorings homes for sale include single-family properties, villas and condominiums with direct access to the Gulf of Mexico. Moorings is a distinguished and serene beachfront community just north of Old Naples. The Moorings real estate shines on quiet streets with beautiful canopied trees.

This exclusive neighborhood is highly sought-after for its desirable location near some of the best shopping, dining and entertainment in Naples. The Moorings luxury homes for sale afford residents a relaxed environment to enjoy nautical, recreational and lifestyle amenities both inside the gates and in the surrounding area.
