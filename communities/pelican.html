About the Neighborhood

A Community Like No Other

Situated over several sprawling acres of breathtaking natural beauty, Pelican Bay encompasses a spectacular selection of Naples real estate. From brilliant high-rises with enchanting views of the Gulf to exquisite estates and delightful condominiums, Pelican Bay properties represent a beautiful blend of luxury and sophistication. These remarkable residences are enhanced by picturesque scenery and the myriad of amenities available for enjoyment. If you’re seeking a luxury home in Naples yet crave an exceptional lifestyle, Pelican Bay is your key to paradise.
