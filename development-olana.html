Welcome to Olana

Olana reimagines beachfront condo living as ultra-private, single-family-scale estates. Just 12 residences - two per floor—span roughly 10,000+ SF with ~80 feet of unobstructed Gulf frontage, floor-to-ceiling glass, and seamless indoor-outdoor living. Thoughtfully conceived so most amenities live inside your home (wellness spa, theater, club-style spaces), Olana delivers a low-density, hyper-private lifestyle on one of Naples’ most coveted stretches of sand.

Highlights & Amenities

  • 10,000+ SF five-bedroom floor plans; seven baths + two powder rooms (select plans).
  • 80' of direct Gulf frontage per residence; floor-to-ceiling glass.
  • In-residence wellness spa (steam/sauna), sound-insulated theater, wine room, club/game rooms.
  • Ground-floor homes with private pools and direct beach access.
  • Ultra-low density: 12 homes, two per floor.
