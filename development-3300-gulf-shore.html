Welcome to 3300 Gulf Shore

A boutique twin-tower address by Kolter Urban, 3300 Gulf Shore is planned for just 51 residences with sweeping bay and Gulf vistas, resort-style amenities, and a private marina - delivering a rare blend of coastal calm and walkable Naples convenience.

Highlights & Amenities

  • Two boutique 8-story buildings; 51 residences.
  • Private marina with ~16 slips; scenic bay-to-Gulf boating.
  • Resort amenities include a 75' lap pool (plus additional planned offerings).
  • Waterfront setting with bay and Gulf views.
